ABOUT US

OUR STORY

That is the story that many have spoken about who we are and what we do. The history of SPRING COMMERCE is a tale of success, as we are a group of fashion enthusiasts who seize opportunities at the right time, coupled with a far-reaching vision to develop and elevate the company. Starting from our first small shop in Ho Chi Minh City in 2023, we have witnessed and brought about many exciting changes in the Vietnamese fashion industry.

OUR COMMITMENTS

  • Spring Commerce provides the highest quality products and services to consumers.
  • We develop a range of convenient services alongside providing fashionable clothing.
  • We build and expand our stores with the best products.
  • We work tirelessly to improve our customers' fashion sense in every location, and for Vietnamese consumers in general.
  • We continuously innovate to better meet consumers' needs.

OUR VISION

  • We believe it is our mission to ensure beauty for consumers and help them experience beauty in every product.
  • Beautiful clothing in general, and product quality in particular, can make people more positive in life. Ensuring the best-looking and best-priced products is our duty.
  • We have confidence in what we are doing based on standards in creating beautiful fashion designs, product quality, and customer care.

SPRING COMMERCE'S PROFESSIONAL TEAM
